SkillNJob Official Website

SkillNJob is a mobile application that helps students and job seekers discover verified internships, job opportunities, and learning resources. We collect publicly available listings and direct users to the official source for each post.

About the App

  • Browse internships and job updates with verified official links.
  • Filter and explore listings easily.
  • Lightweight app with fast navigation.
  • No login or signup required.

Non-affiliation Disclaimer

SkillNJob is not a government app. We are not affiliated with any government entity. All listings are aggregated from publicly available sources.
